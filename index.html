Meet Picasso!

He is such a good boy, that I'm making a website for him - which I know he'll actively appreciate and think about on a regular basis.

Picasso's nicknames include but are not limited to: Pico, Pico Beans, Pablo, Pikachu, Paco, Mr. Beans, P, Mr. Spinch, Pink Baby and Mr. Smelly.

Pico's Hobbies

Staring

There are no limits to what Paco can stare at - me while I'm sleeping, Tim in the bathroom, the wall, the ceiling, a closet door.

Going on Car Rides

One of the few times P runs is when he's moving towards a vehicle. All of his dreams come true inside of a car - any car.

Eating Pudding

It's one of his favorite foods. If we let him, Picasso would eat pudding until he keeled over. We use pudding to lure him into getting his nails trimmed.

More About Pico

Bedtime

To go to sleep, he requires multiple blankets and at least two toys in his bed.

Hide and Seek

He's very good at hiding, mainly when it rains. He comes with a built-in tornado response system.

Dress-Up

Wearing clothes sometimes makes him sad, but he looks so cute. Also, winters in the Midwest are no joke.
